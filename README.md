# Automatic-PTT
Automatically presses the PTT (Push-To-Talk) key via mic activity.

# Why did I make this?
Because the only other program I could find that does this is "autoptt", which sucks. So I decided to give people something they deserve.

I give my word that my software:
- Will never periodically close every so often to beg for money.
- Be free for all, forever, valuing the user above mere monetary value.

And I am in the process of implementing other featurs, such as:
- Push-To-Mute (and a global version of push to mute)
- Toggle Mute
- Normal Push-To-Talk (for things that do not have PTT)
- Toggles for mute and open mic
- And more

# Will this record conversations or data?
No. I, and my software, does not, and will not, ever record, save, or otherwise asses or view any of your data or conversations. All it does is detect mic activity, and press a key.

# Final notes
Finally, I will not ever ask for any monetary value for my work. I strive to provide a free, quality service that competes, and hopefully beats the competition (no matter how money hungry and good they may be)
If you have any suggestions, feel free to put them in as an issue. I will work to add logs in the future for better debugging, bug fixes, and improvements.
